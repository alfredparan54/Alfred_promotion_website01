About Lala

Lala is a 2nd class municipality in the province of Lanao del Norte, Philippines. Known for its coastal beauty and rich cultural heritage, Lala offers a unique blend of natural attractions and warm hospitality.

With a population of over 70,000 people, Lala is a thriving community with agriculture and fishing as its main industries. The town is also becoming known for its eco-tourism potential.

Lala was officially created as a municipality on July 4, 1959, through Executive Order No. 350 issued by President Carlos P. Garcia.
