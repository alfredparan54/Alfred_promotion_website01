Plan Your Visit

Boracay Island

Boracay is famous for its stunning coastlines, powdery white sand, many islands, crystal clear waters, and beautiful sunset, making it one of the top islands in the Philippines. It has won multiple awards in previous years.

Learn More

Crystal Cove Island

Crystal Cove has long been a source of inspiration for plein air painters, a type of landscape painting that originated in France. Early plein air painters documented Orange County's coastline, and Crystal Cove, in particular, with their paintings.

Learn More

parawing

Paraw sailing is a popular activity in Boracay, which is located in Aklan. These boats are often used for island hopping and recreational trips.
